3 / Theme

Cross-disciplinary sustainable innovation with agentic and physical AI.

What sparks will fly when agentic AI meets physical, embodied intelligence — and both are asked to serve a world that has to last?

TAAI 2026 is jointly hosted by the Taiwanese Association for Artificial Intelligence and National Taipei University. This year we're inviting researchers, practitioners, and policy minds to step off the usual circuit and into a forest — to look hard at what cross-domain, sustainable AI actually looks like when you build it, deploy it, and live with its consequences.
