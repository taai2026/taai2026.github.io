Conference Theme

Agentic AI meets Physical AI

The 31st International Conference on Technologies and Applications of Artificial Intelligence (TAAI 2026), hosted by National Taipei University (NTPU), will be held on November 20–21, 2026, at The Great Roots Forestry Spa Resort, New Taipei City, Taiwan.

The conference theme, “Agentic AI and Physical AI for Cross-Domain Sustainable Innovation,” highlights the growing convergence of autonomous AI agents, embodied intelligence, and real-world AI applications. TAAI 2026 aims to foster interdisciplinary exchange and innovation across research, industry, and society toward a more sustainable future.

3 / Theme

Cross-disciplinary sustainable innovation with agentic and physical AI.

What sparks will fly when agentic AI meets physical, embodied intelligence — and both are asked to serve a world that has to last?

TAAI 2026 is jointly hosted by the Taiwanese Association for Artificial Intelligence (TAAI) and National Taipei University (NTPU). This year we're inviting researchers, practitioners, and policy minds to step off the usual circuit and into a forest — to look hard at what cross-domain, sustainable AI actually looks like when you build it, deploy it, and live with its consequences.
