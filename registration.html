03 / Policies

Cancellations, transfers, and fine print.

Cancellation

Until 30 Sep 2026
Full refund minus 10% processing fee.
1 – 31 Oct 2026
50% refund.
After 1 Nov 2026
No refund, but registration is transferable to a colleague at no extra cost.

Good to know

  • Accepted-paper authors: at least one author must register by 1 Sep 2026 for the paper to appear in proceedings.
  • Name changes (transfers) are free up to one week before the conference.
  • Invoices for institutional reimbursement are issued in New Taiwan Dollars only.
  • Visa letters of invitation available on request after registration.
  • All attendees agree to abide by the TAAI Code of Conduct — published on the conference website.
